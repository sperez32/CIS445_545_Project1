Biography

Sebastian is currently studying towards a Bachelor's Degree at Bradley University in Peoria, Illinois in Computer Information Systems with a Minor in Cybersecurity. At Bradley University, he is involved with Bradley University's Club Baseball Team and serves on the board as the team Treasurer.

In 2020, he graduated from De La Salle Insitute in Chicago, Illinois with an Honors Diploma and was involved with Baseball, Golf, Boxing, and Robotics Club.

Sebastian grew up in the South Suburbs of Chicago in Bridgeview, Illinois, where he spent his whole life and still resides.
